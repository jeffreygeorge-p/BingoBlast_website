One of the most played games worldwide is bingo. It's purely a chance and luck game with no skill requirements. A lottery game known as "Il Giuoco del Lotto d'Italia" was played in Italy in the 1500s, and bingo is one type of it. After years of being played in halls, bingo has now been effectively converted to an internet game, which each month attracts millions of gamers worldwide.
